Ashneer Grover

Co-founder of BharatPe | Entrepreneur | Investor

Biography

Ashneer Grover is an Indian entrepreneur and business executive known for his significant contributions to the fintech industry. Born in 1982 in Delhi, he completed his schooling at the prestigious St. Columba's School. He then went on to earn a Bachelor’s degree in Commerce from the University of Delhi and an MBA from the Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Ahmedabad, one of the top business schools in India.

Before co-founding BharatPe in 2018, Ashneer worked with several esteemed organizations. He started his career at the prestigious investment bank, Kotak Investment Bank, where he honed his skills in financial services. He later joined the global consulting firm, Bain & Company, where he was involved in advising various startups and corporate clients.

In his role as the co-founder of BharatPe, Ashneer played a pivotal role in revolutionizing the way small merchants accept payments in India. BharatPe provides a seamless and efficient platform that enables merchants to accept digital payments through QR codes, and it also offers them access to credit and other financial services. Under Ashneer’s leadership, BharatPe quickly grew to become one of the most prominent fintech startups in India, raising significant funding from top venture capitalists.

Ashneer gained immense popularity as a judge on the Indian version of the reality show "Shark Tank," where he showcased his expertise in identifying and nurturing promising startups. His blunt yet insightful feedback resonated with both entrepreneurs and viewers alike, further establishing him as a key figure in the Indian entrepreneurial ecosystem.

In addition to his professional achievements, Ashneer is known for his advocacy for financial literacy and his commitment to empowering small businesses across India. He frequently shares insights and advice through social media, motivating young entrepreneurs to pursue their dreams and innovate in the financial sector.

Through his journey, Ashneer Grover has proven to be a visionary leader, transforming the landscape of digital payments in India and leaving an indelible mark on the startup ecosystem.

Business Vision

Ashneer Grover believes in democratizing financial services for small and medium businesses in India. His business strategy focuses on providing solutions that bridge the gap between traditional finance and the fast-evolving digital landscape. Under his leadership, BharatPe has empowered millions of merchants with easy access to payments and credit.

Key Achievements

As the co-founder of BharatPe, Ashneer Grover has led the company to achieve significant milestones, including securing funding from top venture capital firms. He is also recognized for his role on Shark Tank India, where he invested in and mentored promising startups.
